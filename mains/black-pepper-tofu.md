# Black Pepper Tofu

## Ingredients

+ 3 cloves garlic
+ 1/4 yellow onion
+ 2-3 stalks green onion
+ .5in knob ginger
+ 1 cup vegetable broth
+ 60g soy sauce (5 tbsp)
+ 12g granulated sugar (1 tbsp)
+ .5 tsp MSG
+ 20g potato starch
+ 20g corn starch
+ 1 block tofu
+ 1 egg
+ 7g potato starch
+ 2 tsp black pepper

## Instructions

### Tofu
1. Drain and rinse the block of tofu.
2. Slice it into .5 inch cubes.
3. Place it into a salt water brine for 15-30 minutes.
4. Rinse, Drain, ands Place tofu onto a clean paper towel in one layer to drain.
5. Prepare dredgeing setup with 1 beaten egg in a bowl and a 50/50 mix of corn starch and potato startch in another.
6. Dip each tofu into the egg, then the dry mixture and place onto a parchment lined sheet-pan.
7. Spray tofu with cooking oil and cook in oven at 400 deg F until it browned all over (30 min).

### Sauce
1. Slice the onion, green onion (whites), and garlic.
2. Stir fry the aromatics with 1 tbsp of a neutral oil.
3. Once softened and fragrant mix in sauce with: veg broth, soy sauce, sugar, black pepper, grated ginger, and MSG.
4. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer for a few minutes until all the sugar is disolved.
5. When tofu is ready, toss into the sauce and turn back onto a simmer.
6. Make a small slurry with the 7g of potato starch and mix in to thicken the sauce.
7. Finish off with sliced green onion (greens).
